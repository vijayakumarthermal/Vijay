About Me

Dr. Thilagan K. is a dedicated researcher specializing in solar energy and desalination systems. With a Ph.D. from IIT Madras, his work focuses on innovative solutions for sustainable water purification using renewable energy. He has published extensively in high-impact journals and presented at international conferences. Dr. Thilagan is a member of prestigious organizations like the International Solar Energy Society and the Indian Desalination Association.
